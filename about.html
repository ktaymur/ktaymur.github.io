About the Creator

Hi, friends! My name is Emily, and I am the creator and owner of Blue Strawberry Studio. I began practicing hand lettering back in 2016, and trust me when I say: I was no good. With lots of practice and love for my newfound hobby, I improved significantly. Around that time, I started to experiment with watercolors and watercolor lettering as well. I practiced for multiple years and found creating art to be very therapeutic and stress-relieving. In June of 2018, I decided to open an Etsy Shop to sell my original watercolor lettering pieces. At this point, I also created my Blue Strawberry Studio Instagram account in order to promote my artwork and Etsy Shop. While my pieces weren't overly successful, I enjoyed creating them and I was dedicated to growing my shop and Instagram.

At the start of 2019, I was gifted an iPad Pro and an Apple Pencil, and it was then that I discovered the popular Procreate App. I quickly fell in love with digital lettering and started to create my own Procreate brushes, stamps, and other items. In mid-2020, during quarantining for the Coronavirus, I released my first digital products to my Etsy Shop and my life has been changed ever since. In 2021, I became the owner of my beloved Cricut Machine. I used this to make my first physical products: stickers! After I expanded my sticker shop for a few months, I transitioned to other products as well, such as bookmarks, notepads, and planners. I am proud to say I love my job, and each new order never fails to bring my joy!
