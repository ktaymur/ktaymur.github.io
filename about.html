About the Creator

My name is Emily and I am based out of Apex, NC. I began practicing hand lettering over five years ago and started watercolor soon after. I find creating art to be very therapeutic. About two years ago was when I bought an iPad Pro and discovered the popular Procreate App! I quickly fell in love with digital lettering and started to create Procreate brushes, kits, and stamps to help others with their digital lettering journey! My goal is to help others improve their own digital creations!
